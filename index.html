How we met

We met at Hooters! Jannard was finishing his Senior Year at UIUC, and Hannah was working as a preschool teacher in Champaign. Before Jannard graduated friends wanted to come visit him. On that visit, they wanted to watch the Pacquiao - Bradley II fight, and their friends invited Hannah to join them for wings. The night ended with Jannard and Hannah becoming friends.

On 11.15.2014 - Jannard and Hannah began their relationship.

Fast forward to 02.24.2019 - Jannard surprises Hannah with a 30th birthday party surrounded by family and friends. The best gift that night was gaining a fiancé!

Hannah and Jannard are living the busy city life in Chicago. Jannard is a Implementation Architect at Salesforce. Hannah is a Registered Nurse at North Shore University Evanston Hospital. Both are busy planning for our next adventure, the wedding! We cannot wait to see you on our BIG day!
